Coconut Burfi

Ingredients:-
  • 1 Full Coconut
  • 50gm Vermicelli
  • 50ml Soya milk
  • 200ml Milkmaid
  • 40gm Butter
  • Dry fruits
  • One finely chopped apple (optional)
  • 1 table spoon powdered Cardamom
Preparation Steps:-
  • Take coconut pieces and grate it using a grater.
  • On a medium flame fry the vermicelli with butter till it turns golden. Put it aside.
  • Fry grated coconut in butter for 2 minutes.
  • Add fried vermicelli, condensed milk. Keep frying on low - medium flame.
  • Add soya milk.
  • Add powdered cardamom, mix it properly. Add apple and dry fruits. Remove from flame in 3 minutes after it becomes dry (water content is evaporated).
  • Take the mixture to a flat plate, decorate with dry fruits.
  • Refrigerate for an hour or two (better over night). Burfi is ready!!! Cut it into small cubes and serve.
